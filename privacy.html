Privacy Policy - WhatsApp Translator Pro

Last updated: February 14, 2026

WhatsApp Translator Pro ("we", "our", or "the Extension") respects and protects the privacy of all users ("you" or "user") who use our browser extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Extension.

1. Information Collection

  • The Extension only reads chat messages on WhatsApp Web locally (on your browser) for real-time translation purposes. We do NOT upload any chat content to our servers or any third-party services.
  • We store your translation preferences (e.g., target language, translation switch status) and login credentials locally in your browser to improve user experience (you don’t need to reconfigure settings when reopening the Extension).

2. Information Use

  • Collected information is only used to provide real-time translation functionality for WhatsApp Web. It is not used for any other purposes.
  • We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Contact Customer Service" feature in the Extension.
